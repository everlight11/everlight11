About Us

Education for life

Everlight College is a mixed day and boarding school located in Ggaba Bunga in Kampala. Everlight College would like to partner with parents and guardians to nurture our children into wholesome adults who know God and are committed to making Him known (John 17:3). It is our belief at Everlight College, that God placed us here on earth for a reason: to know him, to relate to him and in so doing become more like him and as we dedicate our lives in service to him;to bring those he puts us in contact with, to know him. As Christians, we are compelled to help our students realize the true purpose of life. Education is a partnership between home and school – parents and teachers. At Everlight College, we desire to work with teachers who live by example, relate to students and guide them with gentleness, as they impart knowledge, skills, discipline, and train them for life. We aim to create a school environment in which students relate cordially with fellow students and with their teachers in order to learn and grow together as a team. We provide activities that enable students to learn from one another and to encourage each other to be the very best they all can be. In our focus on life, we go beyond academic excellence and give opportunities to students to apply the knowledge they acquire in school; to discover what God placed in them and how best to use it for His glory and so be prepared for life in a wholesome manner; develop their God-given talents and prepare for all aspects of life as responsible adults – career, marriage, parenting and impact on society.

Our Vision

Everlight College is a Christian educational community that graduates students marked by academic excellence, personal competence, and marketable skills.

Our Mission

To encourage independent and creative thinking, to provide a mastery of essential knowledge, still moral and spiritual values having regard to quality and discipline.

Student Qualities

It is our prayer, our goal and our purpose to nurture students to possess the following qualities:

  • Love for God, - have faith in him and seek to honor him in everything they do
  • Academic excellence – do the best they can in their academic work
  • Personal Integrity – make a choice to do what is right no matter the circumstances
  • Appreciation and preservation of nature and the environment
  • Self-confidence – courage to do what they know to be right
  • Morally upright – shunning the temptations of youthfulness
  • Humility – take advice, learn from others and be willing to try again when they fail
  • Hard work – it is the price they pay for success
  • Respect for themselves and other people
  • Independent thinkers not afraid to be different
  • Independent thinkers not afraid to be different
  • Courage to try, humility to win gracefully
  • Believe in humanity, develop good relationships and build community
  • Knowledge and wisdom – read, learn.

Activities and facilities

At Everlight College, we seek to teach students to excel as best they can in their individual capabilities. We realize that children have varying degrees of academic capability. In our desire to educate for life, we would like to encourage all students to do their best at what they do best. We therefore consider other skills and developing God-given talents as vital to education as increasing academic knowledge. There are other skills that would enable our student to be fulfilled in life, to be job creators and responsible citizens. We believe that opportunity makes all the difference.

  • Mentoring in business
  • Developing skills in handiwork, design, art and decor, writing, culinary skills.
  • Public speaking.

Our academic facilities include a functional computer lab, a knowledge rich library and an equipped science laboratory.
